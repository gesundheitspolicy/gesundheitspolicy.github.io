Welcome to Gesundheitspolicy! The blog's name derives from Gesundheit (sans exclamation point), German for health and, well, the English policy.

Why not stick with one language, you may wonder. For one, the domain healthpolicy.com was already taken, and there really is no crisp German word for policy or health policy (a few cumbersome analogs exist). The perhaps more compelling reason is that I hope the blog's purpose to come true to its bilingual name: while Gesundheitspolicy will focus on health care in the US, it will draw on my journey from outsider to (budding) insider in the health care system, including the occasional post about health care in Austria, my home country.

So who is this dilettante unable to stick to one language?

My name is Max Pany and I am currently a medical and graduate student at Harvard University.
